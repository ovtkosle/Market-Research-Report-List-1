<p><p>The dual-arm wafer handling robot market consists primarily of two types: atmospheric robots and vacuum robots. Atmospheric robots operate by manipulating wafers in open air, often using mechanical grips and ensuring minimal contamination. In contrast, vacuum robots utilize suction mechanisms to securely hold and transport wafers in controlled environments, enhancing cleanliness and reducing damage risks. Both types play critical roles in semiconductor manufacturing, aiding in efficient wafer handling while addressing distinct operational requirements and environmental conditions.</p></p>
