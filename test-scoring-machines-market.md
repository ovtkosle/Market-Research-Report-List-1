<p><p>Test scoring machines are categorized into two main types: single side reading and dual side reading. Single side reading machines scan responses from one side of the paper, often used for simpler assessments where speed and efficiency are prioritized. Conversely, dual side reading machines can capture responses from both sides, allowing for more comprehensive evaluations and the ability to process complex answer sheets. This dual capability enhances data processing efficiency and accuracy, catering to varied testing environments and requirements.</p></p>
