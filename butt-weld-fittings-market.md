<p><p>The butt weld fittings market comprises various types, including elbows, tees, reducers, caps, and others. Elbows facilitate directional changes in piping systems, while tees allow for branching connections. Reducers connect pipes of differing diameters, ensuring smooth transitions in flow. Caps are used to seal the ends of pipes, preventing leakage. Other fittings may include flanges and plugs, which serve specialized functions. Together, these components are crucial in industries like oil and gas, construction, and plumbing, ensuring efficient and reliable piping systems.</p></p>
