<p><p>The External Ventricular Drainage System market comprises several key types, including valves, shunts, and external drainage systems. Valves facilitate the controlled flow of cerebrospinal fluid, while shunts redirect fluid from the ventricles to another body cavity, preventing pressure buildup. External drainage systems collect and manage cerebrospinal fluid outside the body, providing monitoring and therapeutic benefits. Other products may include accessories and consumables used in conjunction with these systems, catering to diverse clinical needs in neurosurgery and critical care.</p></p>
