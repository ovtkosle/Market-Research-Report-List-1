<p><p>The radiant cooling system market is categorized primarily by installation surfaces: floor, wall, and ceiling. Floor installation surfaces utilize embedded tubing or panels beneath the flooring to distribute cool temperatures throughout the space, optimizing comfort. Wall installation surfaces incorporate similar technology within walls, allowing for efficient cooling in limited floor spaces. Ceiling installation surfaces use radiant panels or tubing installed in ceilings, effectively cooling the room as heat rises. Each type offers unique benefits in terms of aesthetics, efficiency, and space utilization.</p></p>
