<p><strong>The Chemiluminescence Imaging System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Chemical for Chemiluminescence</li><li>Biochemical for Bioluminescence</li><li>Electromagnetic as Electroluminescence</li><li>Photons as Photoluminescence</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Chemiluminescence Imaging System market encompasses various types based on the luminescence mechanism. Chemical chemiluminescence utilizes chemical reactions to emit light, while biochemical luminescence relies on biological processes, such as reactions in living organisms. Electromagnetic luminescence, or electroluminescence, occurs when materials emit light upon electrical stimulation. Lastly, photoluminescence involves the absorption of photons, which then re-emit light. Each type serves distinct applications in research, diagnostics, and environmental monitoring, enhancing detection capabilities across multiple fields.</p></p>
