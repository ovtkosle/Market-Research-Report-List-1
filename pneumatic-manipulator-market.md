<p><strong>The Pneumatic Manipulator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hanging</li><li>Column</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pneumatic manipulator market comprises two primary types: hanging and column manipulators. Hanging manipulators are suspended from overhead structures and are designed for vertical movement, allowing for efficient handling of large items in production lines. Column manipulators, on the other hand, are mounted on a fixed vertical structure, providing stability and supporting a range of horizontal and vertical movements. Both types enhance operational efficiency and safety in various industrial applications by facilitating precise material handling and reducing manual labor.</p></p>
