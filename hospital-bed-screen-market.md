<p><strong>The Hospital Bed Screen Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Three-fold</li><li>Four-fold</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hospital bed screen market is primarily categorized into two types: three-fold and four-fold screens. Three-fold screens consist of three connected panels, offering a compact solution for patient privacy and flexible room arrangements. In contrast, four-fold screens feature four panels, providing enhanced coverage and flexibility, ideal for larger spaces or when multiple patients need separation. Both types are essential in healthcare settings to maintain patient dignity and ensure a conducive environment for treatment and recovery.</p></p>
