<p><strong>The 3D Imaging TOF Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct ToF (dToF)</li><li>Indirect ToF (iToF)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D Imaging Time-of-Flight (ToF) sensor market consists of two primary types: Direct ToF (dToF) and Indirect ToF (iToF). dToF sensors measure the time it takes for light to travel to an object and back, providing high accuracy and resolution for short-range applications. In contrast, iToF sensors utilize a modulation approach, capturing phase shifts of reflected light, which allows for longer-range measurements and greater resilience to ambient light interference. Both technologies cater to various applications, including robotics and depth sensing.</p></p>
