<p><strong>The UV Laser Diode Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Mode</li><li>Multi-mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The UV laser diode market is categorized into single-mode and multi-mode types based on their emission capabilities. Single-mode diodes emit a single wavelength of light, providing high precision and coherence, making them ideal for applications like high-resolution printing and sensing. In contrast, multi-mode diodes produce multiple wavelengths, offering higher power output and broader beam profiles suited for applications such as material processing and curing. The choice between the two depends on specific application requirements for power, precision, and energy efficiency.</p></p>
