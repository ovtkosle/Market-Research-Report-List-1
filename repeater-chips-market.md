<p><strong>The Repeater Chips Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li> Retimer</li><li>Redriver </li></ul></p>
<p><p>The repeater chips market encompasses two primary types: retimers and redrivers. Retimers restore signal integrity by regenerating data and clock signals, ensuring reliable data transmission over long distances, commonly used in high-speed applications like data centers. Redrivers, on the other hand, amplify and equalize signals to extend transmission distances without significant degradation, often utilized in consumer electronics and communication systems. Both play crucial roles in enhancing performance and connectivity in modern digital ecosystems.</p></p>
