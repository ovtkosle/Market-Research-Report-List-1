<p><p>The Polyphenylene Sulfide (PPS) fibers market is primarily segmented into two types: PPS filaments and PPS staple fibers. PPS filaments are continuous strands used in applications requiring high strength and heat resistance, such as filtration and automotive components. Conversely, PPS staple fibers are short strands utilized in non-woven fabrics and insulation materials. Both types harness the inherent chemical stability and thermal resistance of PPS, making them ideal for demanding environments across various industries, including textiles, aerospace, and electronics.</p></p>
