<p><p>The Leaded Multilayer Ceramic Capacitors (MLCC) market is segmented into two main types: Radial Lead Type and Axial Lead Type. Radial Lead Type capacitors feature leads extending from the same side, allowing for efficient PCB mounting with limited space, making them suitable for compact electronic devices. In contrast, Axial Lead Type capacitors have leads extending from opposite ends, facilitating versatile placement and often higher voltage applications. Both types cater to diverse industries, including telecommunications, automotive, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
