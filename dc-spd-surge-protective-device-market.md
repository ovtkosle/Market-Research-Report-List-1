<p><p>The DC Surge Protective Device (SPD) market comprises two primary types: solid-state DC SPDs and hybrid DC SPDs. Solid-state DC SPDs utilize semiconductor technology to manage transient voltages, providing rapid response times and high reliability for sensitive electronic systems. In contrast, hybrid DC SPDs combine both solid-state components and traditional surge protection elements, offering enhanced performance and versatility across a wider range of applications. These devices are essential for safeguarding solar power systems, electric vehicles, and other DC-driven technologies from voltage surges.</p></p>
