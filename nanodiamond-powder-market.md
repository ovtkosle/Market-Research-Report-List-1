<p><p>Nanodiamond powder is a nanomaterial composed of diamond particles typically ranging from 4 to 100 nanometers in size. The material is produced through detonation synthesis or high-pressure, high-temperature (HPHT) methods, offering unique properties that bridge the gap between traditional diamonds and nanotechnology.</p><p>The Nanodiamond Powder Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.7% during the forecast period, driven by increasing demand across industries such as healthcare, electronics, and manufacturing. In electronics, they are utilized in thermal management solutions and as additives in composite materials to enhance performance. The market is also benefiting from advancements in production techniques, which are reducing costs and improving scalability.</p><p>Key trends include the development of functionalized nanodiamonds for specialized applications, such as quantum computing and sensors, and the growing adoption of sustainable manufacturing practices.
