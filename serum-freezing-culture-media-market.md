<p><p>The Serum Freezing Culture Media market is categorized into two primary types: DMSO and DMSO-free. DMSO-based media serve as cryoprotectants, effectively preventing cellular damage during freezing and thawing processes. They are commonly used in various cell types, including stem cells. In contrast, DMSO-free media cater to the growing demand for alternative solutions, minimizing potential toxicity and immunogenicity concerns associated with DMSO. Both types are crucial for cell preservation in research and therapeutic applications, ensuring cell viability.</p></p>
