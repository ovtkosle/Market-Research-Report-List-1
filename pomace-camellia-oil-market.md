<p><p>Pomace Camellia Oil is produced using two primary methods: expelling and lixiviation. The expelling method involves mechanically squeezing the seeds to extract oil, retaining more nutrients and flavor. This process is often considered more natural and less processed. In contrast, lixiviation utilizes solvents to dissolve and extract oil from the pomace, which can lead to higher yields but may involve chemical residues. Both methods cater to different market demands, focusing on quality, efficiency, and production costs.</p></p>
