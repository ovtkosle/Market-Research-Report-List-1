In contrast, alcohol-free sanitizers use alternative active ingredients like benzalkonium chloride, offering a gentler option for sensitive skin, though they may be less effective against certain pathogens. The choice between these types often depends on factors like user preference, skin sensitivity, and specific sanitizing needs.</p></p>
