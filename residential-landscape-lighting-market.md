<p><ul><li>Low Voltage</li><li>Line Voltage</li><li>Solar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The residential landscape lighting market features three main types: low voltage, line voltage, and solar lighting. Low voltage systems use transformers to reduce power, providing safe, energy-efficient lighting ideal for gardens and pathways. Line voltage lighting operates at standard electrical current, offering brighter illumination for larger spaces but requiring professional installation for safety. Solar lighting harnesses sunlight for energy, providing an eco-friendly and easily installable option, though its brightness and effectiveness may vary depending on weather conditions.</p></p>
