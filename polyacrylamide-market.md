<p><p>The polyacrylamide market is categorized into several types based on charge properties. Non-Ionic Polyacrylamide (PAMN) is neutral and widely used in diverse applications such as water treatment. Anionic Polyacrylamide (APAM) carries a negative charge, making it effective for thickening and flocculating in mining and wastewater treatment. Cationic Polyacrylamide (CPAM) has a positive charge, enhancing its use in paper manufacturing and sludge dewatering. The "Others" category includes various specialized formulations tailored for specific industrial needs, expanding the market's versatility.</p></p>
