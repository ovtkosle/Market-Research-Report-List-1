<p><ul><li>Electro Chemical Energy Storage System</li><li>Mechanical Energy Storage System</li><li>Thermal Storage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial Energy Storage System market comprises three main types: </p><p>1. **Electrochemical Energy Storage Systems** utilize batteries, such as lithium-ion, to store and release electrical energy efficiently.</p><p>2. **Mechanical Energy Storage Systems** involve methods like flywheels and pumped hydro storage, converting energy into mechanical form for later use.</p><p>3. **Thermal Storage Systems** store energy in the form of heat or cold, often using materials like water or molten salt, to manage energy supply and demand. Each type plays a crucial role in enhancing energy reliability and efficiency.</p></p>
