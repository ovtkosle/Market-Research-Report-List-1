<p><p>Reclosure labels are classified into three main market types: PET type, BOPP type, and other materials. BOPP (Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene) type labels are lightweight, provide excellent clarity, and are often used for cosmetics and personal care products. The "other" category encompasses various materials like paper or biodegradable options, catering to niche markets and environmentally conscious consumers. Together, these types ensure versatility in packaging solutions.</p></p>
