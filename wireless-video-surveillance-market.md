<p><strong>The Wireless Video Surveillance Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Analog Microwave Transmission</li><li>Digital Microwave Transmission</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Wireless Video Surveillance market features two primary types of transmission: Analog Microwave Transmission and Digital Microwave Transmission. Analog Microwave Transmission uses traditional radio frequency signals to send video data, typically with lower resolution and more interference susceptibility. In contrast, Digital Microwave Transmission employs advanced encoding techniques, offering higher resolution video and enhanced security through encryption. This latter method is favored for modern surveillance systems due to its superior clarity, reliability, and resistance to eavesdropping, making it increasingly popular in various sectors.</p></p>
