<p><p>The color dispersions market consists of two primary types: water-based and UV-curable dispersions. Water-based color dispersions utilize water as a solvent, making them environmentally friendly and suitable for various applications, including coatings, textiles, and inks. In contrast, UV-curable dispersions employ ultraviolet light to initiate curing, resulting in rapid drying and enhanced durability. Both types cater to different industry needs, balancing performance, environmental impact, and application versatility across sectors such as manufacturing, printing, and automotive.</p></p>
