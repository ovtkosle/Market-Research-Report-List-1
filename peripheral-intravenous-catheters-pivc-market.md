<p><p>The Peripheral Intravenous Catheters (PIVC) market is divided into two main types: ported and non-ported PIVCs. Ported PIVCs feature an access port that allows for the administration of medications or fluids without the need for multiple needle sticks, enhancing patient comfort and reducing infection risk. Non-ported PIVCs, on the other hand, are simpler designs without an access port, primarily used for straightforward intravenous access. Both types serve crucial roles in medical settings, catering to diverse patient care needs.</p></p>
