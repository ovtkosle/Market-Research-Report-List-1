<p><p>The Polysilicon Rod market is segmented by production methods: the Silane Method and the Metallurgical Process. The Silane Method involves chemical vapor deposition, where silane gas decomposes to form high-purity polysilicon rods, ideal for semiconductor and solar industries. The Metallurgical Process refines metallurgical-grade silicon through physical and chemical purification, producing lower-cost polysilicon suitable for solar applications. Both methods cater to different market needs, balancing purity, cost, and application-specific requirements, driving the polysilicon industry's growth across electronics and renewable energy sectors.</p></p>
