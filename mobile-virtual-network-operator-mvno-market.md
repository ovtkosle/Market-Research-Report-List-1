<p><p>Mobile Virtual Network Operators (MVNOs) are companies that provide mobile services without owning the underlying network infrastructure. They fall into three main market types: </p><p>1. **Reseller MVNOs** purchase bulk access from a network operator and sell services under their own brand, often with limited customization.</p><p>2. **Service Operator MVNOs** own some infrastructure elements, allowing for more features and control over services.</p><p>3. **Full MVNOs** possess complete control over their network elements, providing a fully branded experience and independence from host networks.</p></p>
