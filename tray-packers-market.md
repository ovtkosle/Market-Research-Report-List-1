<p><p>Tray packers are essential in packaging industries, categorized into horizontal and vertical types based on their orientation and operation. Horizontal tray packers arrange products in a flat, side-by-side layout, ideal for items requiring stable positioning and easy access. In contrast, vertical tray packers stack products vertically, maximizing space efficiency and facilitating high-speed packing for compact items. Both types cater to varying industry needs, including food, pharmaceuticals, and consumer goods, offering unique advantages in product handling and packaging efficiency.</p></p>
