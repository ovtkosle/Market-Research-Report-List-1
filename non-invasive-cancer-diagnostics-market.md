<p><p>The Non-Invasive Cancer Diagnostics market encompasses various innovative approaches, including Circulating Tumor Cells (CTCs), Circulating Tumor Nucleic Acids (ctNAs), and Exosomes. CTCs are cancer cells shed into the bloodstream, offering insights into tumor characteristics. ctNAs involve analyzing fragments of tumor DNA in circulation for genetic mutations. Exosomes, small vesicles released by cells, carry tumor-derived proteins and RNAs, aiding in biomarker discovery. Together, these technologies enhance early detection, monitoring, and personalized treatment of cancer.</p></p>
