<p><p>The Argon laser market encompasses two primary types: Argon Ion Lasers and Argon Excimer Lasers. Argon Ion Lasers use ionized argon gas to produce continuous wave or pulsed light, commonly employed in medical applications like ophthalmology and various scientific research. In contrast, Argon Excimer Lasers utilize a mixture of noble gases and halogens to generate ultraviolet light, often utilized in excimer laser surgery and semiconductor manufacturing. Both types are integral to advancements in medical, industrial, and research technologies.</p></p>
