<p><strong>The dPCR Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Droplet Digital PCR</li><li>Chip-based Digital PCR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital PCR (dPCR) market is primarily divided into two types: Droplet Digital PCR (ddPCR) and Chip-based Digital PCR. ddPCR utilizes thousands of droplets to compartmentalize PCR reactions, enabling highly sensitive quantification of nucleic acids. In contrast, Chip-based Digital PCR employs microfluidic chips for parallel amplification and detection of nucleic acids, facilitating rapid and efficient processing. Both technologies enhance precision in genomic analysis, supporting applications in research, diagnostics, and personalized medicine.</p></p>
