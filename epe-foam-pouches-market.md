<p><p>EPE foam pouches are categorized based on the density of polyethylene used, impacting their properties and applications. High-Density Polyethylene (HDPE) offers robust strength and durability, suitable for heavy-duty packaging. Medium-Density Polyethylene (MDPE) provides a balance of flexibility and strength, ideal for general-purpose usage. Low-Density Polyethylene (LDPE) is lightweight and flexible, making it perfect for cushioning and protective applications. Linear Low-Density Polyethylene (LLDPE) combines stretchability and strength, enhancing tear resistance and making it versatile for various packaging needs.</p></p>
