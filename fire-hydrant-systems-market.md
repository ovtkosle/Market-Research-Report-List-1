<p><p>The fire hydrant systems market primarily comprises wet barrel and dry barrel fire hydrant systems. Wet barrel systems maintain water in the barrel, making them suitable for warmer climates where freezing is not a concern. Conversely, dry barrel systems feature a vertical valve mechanism, keeping water in the underground supply while preventing freezing in colder areas. Both types are critical for effective firefighting, serving commercial, residential, and industrial sectors, with demand driven by urban development and safety regulations.</p></p>
