<p><ul><li>Ionic PAG</li><li>Non-Ionic PAG</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor photoinitiator market is divided into two main types: Ionic Photoacid Generators (PAGs) and Non-Ionic PAGs. Ionic PAGs release protons upon exposure to light, initiating chemical reactions essential for photolithography processes in semiconductor manufacturing. They are highly effective, providing precise control over photoresist patterns. Non-Ionic PAGs, on the other hand, operate without generating charged ions, offering benefits such as reduced residue and improved stability. Both types cater to specific applications within the semiconductor industry to enhance performance and efficiency.</p></p>
