<p><p>Adhesive surface protection films are categorized based on their material types: Polyethylene (PE), Low-Density Polyethylene (LDPE), Polypropylene (PP), Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC), and others. PP films are known for their strength and resistance to chemicals, while PVC films are valued for their versatility and weather resistance. Other materials may include special composites or biodegradable options. Each type serves specific protective needs across various industries.</p></p>
