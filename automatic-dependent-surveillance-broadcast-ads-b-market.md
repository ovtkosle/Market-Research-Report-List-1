<p><p>Automatic Dependent Surveillance Broadcast (ADS-B) is a surveillance technology used in aviation to enhance situational awareness and safety. The market is divided into three types: ADS-B Out, which broadcasts an aircraft's position and status to ground stations and other aircraft; ADS-B In, which receives and displays information from other aircraft and ground stations; and ADS-B Ground Stations, which collect and relay airborne data to air traffic control. Together, these components improve real-time tracking and communication in the aviation industry.</p></p>
