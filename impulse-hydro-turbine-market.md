<p><p>Impulse hydro turbines are a type of water turbine that utilize the kinetic energy of moving water to generate electricity. Unlike reaction turbines, which operate on the pressure of water, impulse turbines function by directing a high-velocity jet of water onto turbine blades, converting the water's kinetic energy into mechanical energy. This makes them particularly effective for high-head hydroelectric applications, where there is a significant height difference between the water source and the turbine.</p><p>The impulse hydro turbine market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.6% during the forecast period, driven by increasing demand for renewable energy sources and advancements in turbine technology. <p><strong>The Impulse Hydro Turbine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pelton Hydro Turbine</li><li>Cross Flow Hydro Turbine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The impulse hydro turbine market includes Pelton and Cross Flow turbines, both designed to harness energy from flowing water. Pelton turbines utilize a series of cups on a wheel to convert the kinetic energy of high-pressure water jet streams into mechanical energy, ideal for high head sites. In contrast, Cross Flow turbines feature a cylindrical design allowing water to flow through both sides, making them suitable for low to medium head applications. Both types contribute significantly to renewable energy production.</p></p>
