<p><p>The Commercial Vehicle Brake Caliper Market is primarily divided into two types: Single Piston Calipers and Multi-Piston Calipers. Single Piston Calipers are simpler and typically lighter, providing sufficient braking force for smaller vehicles or lighter loads. In contrast, Multi-Piston Calipers feature multiple pistons which enhance braking performance and distribute force more evenly, making them suitable for larger, heavier commercial vehicles. This differentiation allows manufacturers to cater to varying performance requirements and vehicle specifications in the commercial sector.</p></p>
