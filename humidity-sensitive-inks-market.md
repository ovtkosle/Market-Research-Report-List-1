<p><p>The humidity-sensitive inks market includes two main types: humidity-sensitive apochromatic ink (reversible) and humidity-sensitive color spreading ink. Reversible apochromatic inks react to humidity changes by altering their color while retaining their original properties when conditions normalize. In contrast, humidity-sensitive color spreading inks release pigments and spread color in response to humidity, creating dynamic visual effects. Both types cater to specialized applications in packaging, security printing, and creative arts, offering unique functionalities that enhance aesthetic appeal and user interaction.</p></p>
