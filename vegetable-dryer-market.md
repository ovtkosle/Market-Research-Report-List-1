<p><strong>The Vegetable Dryer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conduction Type</li><li>Convection Type</li><li>Radiation Type</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vegetable dryer market is categorized into several types based on the drying mechanism. </p><p>1. **Conduction Type:** This method relies on direct contact with a heat source, allowing vegetables to dry through heat transfer.</p><p>   </p><p>2. **Convection Type:** Utilizing airflow, this type circulates hot air around the vegetables, enhancing moisture removal.</p><p>3. **Radiation Type:** Here, infrared or microwave energy directly heats the vegetables, resulting in fast drying.</p><p>4. **Others:** This includes hybrid systems and alternative methods that don't fit traditional categories, catering to diverse processing needs.</p></p>
