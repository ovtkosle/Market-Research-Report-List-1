<p><p>The custom foot orthotics market comprises three main types: rigid orthosis, soft orthosis, and semi-rigid orthosis. Rigid orthosis provides firm support, primarily for alignment and stability, addressing severe foot issues. Soft orthosis offers cushioning and flexibility, ideal for cushioning discomfort in various conditions. Semi-rigid orthosis combines the benefits of both, delivering moderate support and flexibility to balance comfort and stability. Each type is tailored to individual needs, optimizing foot function and alleviating pain for diverse patient populations.</p></p>
