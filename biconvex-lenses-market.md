<p><p>The biconvex lenses market comprises two primary types: coated and uncoated biconvex lenses. Coated biconvex lenses are treated with anti-reflective or other special coatings to enhance light transmission and reduce glare, making them ideal for high-performance optical applications. In contrast, uncoated biconvex lenses have no additional treatments, offering basic optical functions at a lower cost. Both types are widely used in various industries, including photography, telescopes, and medical devices, catering to different performance and budget requirements.</p></p>
