<p><p>Distributed Strain Sensing (DSS) systems utilize optical fibers to measure strain along their length, with two main market types: single-mode fiber-based DSS and multimode fiber-based DSS. Single-mode fiber systems offer higher sensitivity and longer sensing distances, making them suitable for applications in critical infrastructure and geotechnical monitoring. In contrast, multimode fiber-based DSS systems are typically more cost-effective for shorter distances and less critical applications, providing adequate performance for industrial monitoring and structural health assessments.</p></p>
