<p><strong>The High Temperature Recovery Wheels Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Condensation Rotors</li><li>Hygroscopic Rotors</li><li>Sorption Rotors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high-temperature recovery wheels market consists of three main types: condensation rotors, hygroscopic rotors, and sorption rotors. Condensation rotors utilize cooling and condensation to recover energy from exhaust air, while hygroscopic rotors incorporate materials that absorb and release moisture for enhanced energy efficiency. Sorption rotors employ a combination of adsorption and desorption processes to transfer heat and moisture, optimizing energy recovery in various industrial applications. These technologies are essential for improving energy efficiency and sustainability in ventilation systems.</p></p>
