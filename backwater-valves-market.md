<p><ul><li>Normally Open Backwater Valves</li><li>Normally Closed Backwater Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The backwater valves market is segmented into two main types: normally open and normally closed backwater valves. Normally open backwater valves allow fluid flow under standard conditions and automatically close during backflow events, preventing sewage from entering clean water systems. In contrast, normally closed backwater valves remain shut under normal conditions and open only when specific flow criteria are met, providing control in high-risk flood areas. Both types play critical roles in safeguarding plumbing systems and preventing contamination.</p></p>
