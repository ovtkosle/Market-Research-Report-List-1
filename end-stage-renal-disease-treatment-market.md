<p><p>The End Stage Renal Disease (ESRD) treatment market encompasses three primary types: Hemodialysis, Peritoneal Dialysis, and Kidney Transplantation. Hemodialysis involves filtering blood through a machine, typically performed in a clinic or at home. Peritoneal Dialysis utilizes the lining of the abdomen to filter waste, offering more flexibility and home-based options. Kidney Transplantation involves surgically replacing a diseased kidney with a healthy one from a donor, providing a potential cure for ESRD. Each treatment varies in approach, accessibility, and patient lifestyle impact.</p></p>
