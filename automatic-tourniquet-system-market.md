<p><strong>The Automatic Tourniquet System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pneumatic Tourniquet</li><li>Electric Tourniquet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic Tourniquet System market includes two main types: pneumatic and electric tourniquets. Pneumatic tourniquets utilize compressed air to control pressure and limb occlusion, providing precise inflation and deflation capabilities. They are often preferred in surgical settings for their reliability and ease of use. Electric tourniquets, on the other hand, rely on electric mechanisms to apply pressure, offering programmable settings and enhanced user interface for monitoring. Both types are vital for minimizing blood loss during surgical procedures and trauma care.</p></p>
