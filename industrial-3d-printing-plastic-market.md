<p><strong>The Industrial 3D Printing Plastic Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Powder</li><li>Filament</li><li>Liquid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial 3D printing plastic market comprises three main types: powder, filament, and liquid. Powder is used in processes like Selective Laser Sintering (SLS), offering intricate designs and strong mechanical properties. Filament, commonly associated with Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM), is easy to handle and widely used for prototyping. Liquid refers to resin-based printing technologies, such as Stereolithography (SLA), which enable high-resolution parts with smooth finishes. Each type serves unique applications and has distinct advantages in various industries.</p></p>
