<p><p>The DPT vaccination market consists of three main types: DTaP, Tdap, and DTwP. DTaP (Diphtheria, Tetanus, and Pertussis) is a pediatric vaccine given to children under seven years, providing robust protection against these diseases. Tdap is an adult booster shot that ensures ongoing immunity and is typically administered after age 11. DTwP (Diphtheria, Tetanus, and Whole-cell Pertussis) was an earlier form of the vaccine, utilizing whole-cell pertussis components but has largely been replaced by DTaP due to safety concerns.</p></p>
